  the future of water supplies
no one knows how long the current drought will last. it could be over next
year or it could, as some are predicting, last another 25 years.
whichever it is, we need to start conserving water now.
is a simple, battery operated, five minute timer with
a blue and red lighting sequence. the lights change from blue to red
while showering and flash red during the last 30 seconds.
a 3-minute reduction in shower time, from the average 8 to 5 minutes
could save 6-15 gallons of water per shower per person per day.
is more then a timer, it’s a convenient reminder to
"get out of the shower."
one timer will last for approximately 365 showers or 1 year.
